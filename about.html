About ASC Design Studio

Hello there, my name’s Anthony Crisostomo, a Melbourne based graphic designer specialising in print, branding, and web design.

My strengths are in logo design, typography with a strong emphasis on publication layouts, almost all kinds of print, from invites, stationery, business cards, and brochures to... yes, web design layouts. My tools revolve around Acrobat, Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop.

When I made this website, I could have gone two routes. Take the template option or build it from scratch. I chose the latter, and it was the best decision I made. The learning curve was a bit steep for me, but it was worth it!!!

I am now very comfortable in writing HTML and CSS, and I have a basic understanding of JavaScript. I also took the time to learn CSS pre-processors, I use Susy, Breakpoint, Sass + Compass with a Ruby Based workflow… but I will be moving my workflow to GULP as it is much faster!!! It’s a long road ahead but I’m excited!!!

Anyway, thank you for stopping by, and if you think my style suits your needs, just get in touch for a free quote!
